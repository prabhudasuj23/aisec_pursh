# Security Acknowledgements

This file recognizes individuals and organizations who have responsibly disclosed
security vulnerabilities in the AISec platform or the Pursh demonstration application.

For the vulnerability disclosure process, see [.github/SECURITY.md](../.github/SECURITY.md).

---

## Acknowledgements

No vulnerabilities have been publicly disclosed to date.

Researchers who responsibly disclose a valid finding and agree to coordinated
disclosure will be acknowledged here after the fix is shipped.

---

## Disclosure timeline template

When a disclosure is acknowledged, the following information will be added:

| Date reported | Date fixed | Severity | CVE / Advisory | Reporter | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | YYYY-MM-DD | Critical / High / Medium | CVE-YYYY-NNNNN or N/A | Researcher name (with permission) | Brief description |

---

## Recognition policy

- Researchers are acknowledged only with their explicit written permission.
- We do not offer a bug bounty program at this time (this is a portfolio project).
- All researchers who report valid findings are credited by name or alias per their preference.
- Out-of-scope findings (automated scanner noise without coordination) are not eligible for acknowledgement per [SECURITY.md](../.github/SECURITY.md).
